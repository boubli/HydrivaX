Why choose HydrivaX?

Traditional server OSes are bloated. Standard LXC templates lack management tools. HydrivaX gives you the best of both worlds.

🚀

Aggressively Stripped

No kernel modules, no firmware, no bootloader, no locales. HydrivaX starts at just 346MB on disk and idles at 40MB RAM. Much lighter than Debian or Ubuntu Server.

🧩

On-Demand Modularity

Nothing is pre-installed. Need Docker? Run hx-deploy docker. Need Wireguard? hx-deploy wireguard. Everything is installed on demand with full configuration.

🖥️

First-Class LXC Support

Built from the ground up for Proxmox LXC, solving common containerization headaches (like nesting, permissions, and apparmor) out of the box.
